\section{Most used packages on arXiv.org}\label{sec:packages}
The Top Five packages, used on arXiv.org\footnote{according

\begin{enumerate}

  \item amssymb \label{item:amssymb}
  \item amsmath \label{item:amsmath}

  \item amsfonts
\end{enumerate}
